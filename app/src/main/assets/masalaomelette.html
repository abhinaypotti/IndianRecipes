Masala Omelette Recipe


Ingredients:

1)2 Eggs 

2)1 meduim sized chopped Onion (Pyaj) 

3)1 small sized chopped Tomato (Tamatar) 

4)2 chopped Green Chilli (Hari mirch) 

5)Salt to taste

6)1/2 tsp Garam Masala 

7)A pinch of Curry Powder

8)1/2 tsp Cumin Seed Powder (Jeera) 

9)1 tsp Olive Oil

How to make masala omelette:

1)Beat the eggs thoroughly in a basin.

2)Add the onions, tomatoes and the green chillies. mix thoroughly.

3)Add the garam masala powder, curry powder and salt. mix well.

4)Heat the oil in a non stick pan and add the cumin seeds.fry for a few seconds over a medium flame.

5)Pour the egg mixture and cook on both sides till light brown in colour.( reduce the flame) serve hot.
