Dhokla

Ingredients

1.Gram flour(Besan) - 1 1/2 cup

2.Sugar - 3-4 tsp (Diabetic people do not add this)

3.Oil - 1 tblsp

4.Citric acid - 1/2 tsp

5.soda-bi-carb( Baking Soda) - 1/2 tsp

6.Salt - to taste

7.Lemon juice - 3-4 drops

8.Turmeric Powder - 1/4 tsp

9.Oil - 1 tblsp

10.Cumin seeds - 1/4 tsp

11.Mustard seeds - 1/4 tsp

12.Sesame seeds - 1/4 tsp

13.Asaefoetida - a pinch

14.Curry leaves - 5-6

15.Water - 1/2 cup

16.Sugar - 1 tsp

17.Cilantro - for garnish

18.Grated coconut - for garnish

19.Sev - for garnish 

 Method

1. Mix all the ingredients from batch I in a bowl with water enough to make a thick paste(like idli batter). 

2. Blend it well with a hand blender or with hand. 

3. Steam it for 15 minutes on high flame. 

4. Cut it into square pieces.

5. Tempering: Heat 1 tbsp oil. Add jeera(cumin), rai(mustard seeds), til(sesame seeds), hing(asaefoetida) and karipatta(curry leaves). When it splutters add 1/2 cup water and 1 tsp sugar.

6. Pour this water slowly on dhokla pieces. 

7. Garnish this with fresh coriander, shredded fresh coconut and sev. Serve with green chutney or dhokla chutney.

 

Dhokla Chutney

 

  Ingredients

1,Green Coriander - 1 bunch

2.Green Chillies - as needed

3.Dalia - 1 tblsp

4.Cumin seeds - 1 tsp

5.Sugar - as needed

6.Lemon Juice - as needed

7.Salt - as needed
 
Method

1. Grind all the above ingredients together.

2. Spice/sweetness/sour should be all in equal proportion.
